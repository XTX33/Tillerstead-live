<section class="prose pad" aria-labelledby="privacy-heading">
  <p class="text-mono">Last updated: November 2025</p>
  <h2 id="privacy-heading">How we protect your privacy</h2>
  <p>
    Tillerstead LLC ("Tillerstead," "we," or "us") collects only the information needed to respond to your
    requests, provide accurate estimates, and deliver services responsibly. We do not sell or trade your data.
  </p>

  <h3 id="what-we-collect">What we collect</h3>
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Contact details</strong> such as your name, phone number, email, and project address.</li>
    <li><strong>Project information</strong> like photos, measurements, and descriptions you provide for estimating.</li>
    <li><strong>Technical data</strong> such as IP address and browser type collected by hosting or analytics tools.</li>
  </ul>

  <h3 id="how-we-use">How we use your information</h3>
  <ul>
    <li>To respond to inquiries, schedule consultations, and provide estimates.</li>
    <li>To coordinate work, material orders, and project documentation.</li>
    <li>To improve website performance and service quality.</li>
    <li>To comply with New Jersey licensing and record-keeping requirements.</li>
  </ul>
  <p>
    Information may be shared with trusted partners (such as hosting, email, payment, or financing providers) only when
    necessary to fulfill your request and always under privacy-law compliance. We do not sell personal data.
  </p>

  <h3 id="cookies-analytics">Cookies &amp; analytics</h3>
  <p>
    The site may use basic analytics or performance cookies to understand traffic patterns. Cookies are optional and can
    be disabled in your browser settings; core site content remains accessible without them.
  </p>

  <h3 id="data-retention">Data retention &amp; security</h3>
  <p>
    We keep inquiry and project records only as long as needed for estimating, active work, warranty support, or legal
    obligations. We use reasonable safeguards to protect information you submit, though no online transmission is
    completely secure.
  </p>

  <h3 id="your-rights">Your choices</h3>
  <ul>
    <li>Request corrections to contact or project details we have on file.</li>
    <li>Ask us to delete inquiry information that is no longer needed.</li>
    <li>Opt out of non-essential cookies through your browser settings.</li>
  </ul>

  <h3 id="updates">Policy updates</h3>
  <p>
    We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last
    updated" date. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
  </p>
</section>
