About
I’m Devon Tyler, founder of Tillerstead LLC, a New Jersey home improvement company focused on tile, carpentry, and quality home upgrades. Whether it’s a bathroom remodel, flooring install, or drywall and plaster repair, I take pride in doing the job right and treating every home with care. My approach is simple — clear communication, reliable work, and results that last. I focus on the details that make a difference, so you can feel confident you’re getting solid craftsmanship and a smooth experience from start to finish.

FAQs

  • What should the customer know about your pricing (e.g., discounts, fees)?

    I charge for labor only, so you can provide your own materials or have me handle sourcing and delivery for a fair service fee. You’ll always know exactly what you’re paying for — clean, skilled work done with respect for your home and schedule. No hidden costs, no shortcuts, just craftsmanship and honesty.

  • What education and/or training do you have that relates to your work?

    • Trained through on-site mentorship in residential and light commercial remodels • Experience with Schluter®, RedGard®, and cement board waterproofing systems • Skilled in layout, floor leveling, and substrate prep for long-term tile performance • Continued self-education through manufacturer guides, trade resources, and updated building standards

  • How did you get started doing this type of work?

    The trades have always been part of my life. I started hands-on with layout, prep, and finishing work, and eventually found my passion in tile. There’s something rewarding about taking raw surfaces and turning them into clean, lasting finishes — built with patience, precision, and pride.
